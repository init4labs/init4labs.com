Init4Labs is the product and experimentation side of the wider Init4 ecosystem — bringing together practical app ideas, lightweight digital tools and new concepts shaped by real-world needs. It is also shaped by lived experience: 36 years working in Air Traffic Management, and family life with a young adult daughter with significant special needs and a learning disability.
