Common help topics

  • Reminders are not arriving: check in-app reminders plus iOS notification permissions.
  • Widgets look out of date: open the app to refresh the widget snapshot and allow iOS time to update.
  • Shared Widgit links did not prefill: reopen the app once and check that the shared URL is a valid Widgit Online document link.
  • Can’t edit or delete: review the current PIN and permission mode in Settings.

Before you contact support

  • Include your device model and iOS or iPadOS version.
  • Describe what you expected to happen and what happened instead.
  • If the issue involves reminders or widgets, mention whether the app was recently updated.
